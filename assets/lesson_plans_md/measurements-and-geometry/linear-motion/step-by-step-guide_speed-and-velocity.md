# Step by step guide\_Speed and Velocity

## **Pre-Class Preparation**

Prepare stopwatches, measuring tapes, balls, and recording sheets.

## **Phase 1: Discovery (15 minutes)**

\[SAY\] Today we will learn about speed and velocity.

\[DO\] Distribute materials for rolling ball experiment.

## **Phase 2: Instruction (10 minutes)**

\[SAY\] Speed is the rate of change of distance with time.

\[WRITE\] Speed \= Distance / Time

## **Phase 3: Practice (10 minutes)**

\[SAY\] Let us work through examples together.

## **Phase 4: Assessment (5 minutes)**

\[SAY\] Complete the exit ticket individually.
